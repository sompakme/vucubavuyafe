Kylie Jenner Brings the Glamour in a Glittering Gown With a Plunging Neckline

September 14, 2024
blog

Kylie Jenner wore her most glamorous look yet at Paris Fashion Week.

Yesterday, the cosmetics mogul made a showstopping appearance with her arrival at Paris’s Italian Embassy, where she joined the likes of Michelle Yeoh and Emma Chamberlain to sit front-row at Schiaparelli’s Fall/Winter 2024 runway show.

Jenner turned out in a silvery, body-hugging gown completely covered in glittering sequins. The halterneck piece featured a plunging scoop neckline and a literal keyhole-shaped cutout at the center of her bust. She finished off the look with a pair of diamond-encrusted drop earrings with an eye pendant at the center, plus Schiaparelli’s signature sculpted toe pumps in a nude tone.

This isn’t the first time Jenner has turned heads thanks to Daniel Roseberry’s ingenious Schiaparelli creations.

During Paris Couture Week Spring/Summer 2023 season earlier this year, the mom of two attended the house’s runway show dressed in a black velvet bodycon gown, which featured a strapless neckline, ruched details all along the sides, and a hyperrealistic lion head mounted to her bust. Models later strutted down the runway in a series of similar gowns; Irina Shayk wore a one-shouldered version of Jenner’s taxidermy lion dress, while Naomi Campbell wore a shaggy fur coat dress with a fake wolf’s head resting on her shoulder.

Of the surreal adornments, Roseberry later wrote on Instagram that the animal heads were “celebrating the glory of nature and guarding the woman who wears it.”
