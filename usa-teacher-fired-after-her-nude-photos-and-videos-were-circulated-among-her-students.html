USA: Teacher fired after her nude photos and videos were circulated among her students

September 01, 2024
blog

  • TIRAMILLAS

Actualizado 27/02/2021 - 17:00

A Hebrew school in Miami, Florida has been living through a hugely controversial episode in which a teacher lost her job after her nude photos and videos were circulated among her students.

Alexandra Handwerger, 48, claims she was the unfortunate victim of someone leaking these sensitive images, which she admits were legitimate and taken by herself, to students at the school she previously worked at.

Two weeks after the matter surfaced, Handwerger was dismissed from her post at the school, whilst an investigation is underway in order to ascertain whether or not she herself was the source of the leak.

At present she hasn't been arrested, nor is she facing any charges, although that could change depending on the findings of the investigation.

Irrespective of that, the images have spread like wildfire throughout the school, with the educational facility being forced to send a letter to parents and alumni explaining the situation.

"We do not know how our students received the videos/photos at this time, but we have been and continue to conduct a thorough investigation regarding the entirety of this serious matter," a school statement read.

Jude Faccidomo is acting as the lawyer for Handwerger and has declared the suggestions that the teacher was sending the images to students herself as unfounded, making clear that her client was working in tandem with the authorities to ensure the facts are clarified.
