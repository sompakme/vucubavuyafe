FAQs

Can I get free shipping at Finish Line?

Absolutely! When you spend at least $75 at Finish Line you are eligible for free shipping which is otherwise $8. It’s great to know that once you have filled your basket with your favorite items, you won’t need to pay additional costs.

Can I get a military discount at Finish Line?

Unfortunately, Finish Line does not currently offer military discounts to its customers. However, it has been done in the past so this may well change - we'll be sure to let you know! If you’re looking for another way to save, you can always use one of our handy coupon codes instead! 

How do I sign up for rewards with Finish Line?

Become a STATUS member to earn rewards from Finish Line. It's completely free to join and will grant you access to an exclusive welcome gift, earn points for every dollar you spend online, and more. After earning 5000 points in a calendar year you will be automatically upgraded to A-List status. 

Can I shop the Finish Line sale?

Finish Line runs sales during seasonal periods such as Black Friday and Christmas, but customers can also shop the sale all year long by visiting the 'Sale' section of the Finish Line. You'll be able to find savings of up to 60% throughout the year, no matter the month. 

Does Finish Line offer free returns?

Yes, Finish Line offers free returns provided you return your order within their 45-day returns period. To create a return, head to the Finish Line website returns portal. 

What is the returns policy at Finish Line?

At Finish Line, they have a 45-day returns policy so you'll have more than a month to change your mind. Ensure all items are returned in their original condition with tags attached, to be eligible for a full refund.
