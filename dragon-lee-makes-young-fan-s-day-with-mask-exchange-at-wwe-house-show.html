Dragon Lee Makes Young Fans Day with Mask Exchange at WWE House Show

August 07, 2024
blog

Dragon Lee is definitely one of the most talented pro wrestlers in the business right now. Lee signed with WWE last year and the company has big plans for him. Now Dragon Lee decided to make a young fan’s day with a mask exchange during a WWE live event.

Ringside News exclusively reported that Dragon Lee will be part of the Friday Night Smackdown roster. He would then be officially added to the blue brand afterwards. In fact, Lee also signed a new contract with WWE back in October.

During a WWE live event on December 2 at Allentown, Dragon Lee competed in a match. Lee also had a wholesome interaction with a young fan, as he exchanged masks with the young fan in attendance. Lee ended up wearing a Rey Mysterio mask.

Dragon Lee was named as a participant in a new eight-man tournament to determine who would be the next to get a shot at Logan Paul’s United States Title. We will have to wait and see if Lee will end up becoming the #1 contender in the end.
