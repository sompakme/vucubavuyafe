Viola Davis wins Oscar award after third nomination (photos, video)

August 24, 2024
blog

Viola Davis just won her first Oscar ever for her work in Fences.

Davis will be the first black woman to win three major awards in one year. She had earlier in the year won a Tony and Emmy awards.

While receiving her Oscar award in the Best Supporting Actress category, the 51-year-old actress gave an extremely emotional speech!
