Influencer uses blanket to recreate Kim Kardashian's N92,900 pyjamas

September 08, 2024
blog

- A woman has gone viral on social media for recreating an outfit by Kim Kardashian

- The clothing item is a three-piece pyjama set

- Her outfit was much cheaper than the price of Kim's expensive sleepwear

A woman has created a cheaper alternative for a Kim Kardashian product. The Kardashian fan made her own version of the superstar's shape-wear brand, SKIMS.

Kheumani Stevenson took to social media to share photos of the cute three-piece pyjama set that she made herself.

She made the set from a blanket, which cost about N8,700. The item sold by Kim Kardashian costs up to N92,900.

The influencer posted the outfit on Twitter and inspired many to create the outfit themselves as well.

According to Insider, Stevenson said she aimed to recreate the SKIMS range at cheaper prices. She particularly liked the brand's 'Cozy Collection'.

She shared her sewing process in a video on YouTube.
