Larsa Pippen Divulges On Age Gap With Marcus Jordan

July 30, 2024
blog

In their inaugural episode, Larsa Pippen spoke about the age gap between the two. Larsa is currently 48 years old while Marcus Jordan is 32. However, as she explains, this was never an issue for her. In fact, she has noted that she has dated men who are actually considerably younger. Consequently, none of this fazed her. All she really cared about was whether or not he was a good person and had a solid head on his shoulders.

Larsa Pippen Tells All

LOS ANGELES, CALIFORNIA - OCTOBER 29: Reality TV Personality Larsa Pippen attends the CARN*EVIL Halloween Party at a private residence on October 29, 2022 in Los Angeles, California. (Photo by Paul Archuleta/Getty Images)

“Us doing this podcast, it really was therapeutic for us. Like, ‘Hey, let’s talk about this,’ or, ‘People want to know about our age gap. Should we talk about that?’ Because we really don’t view age like that,” Pippen explained. “I’ve dated guys that are way older than me and I’ve dated guys that are younger than Marcus. So for me, it wasn’t like I was so concerned about a 16-year age difference. I was kind of concerned about what’s going on in your brain. If you’re mature or not, if you feel good about yourself — that, to me, is more important than someone’s age.”
