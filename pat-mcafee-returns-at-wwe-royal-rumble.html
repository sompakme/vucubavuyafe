Pat McAfee Returns At WWE Royal Rumble

September 26, 2024
blog

Pat McAfee made a name for himself in Triple H’s version of NXT, where he feuded with Adam Cole. Vince McMahon was impressed with his work and called him up to SmackDown.

Click here for our full coverage of WWE Royal Rumble.

Pat McAfee made his surprise to WWE during the Royal Rumble tonight. McAfee showed up to a huge pop from the crowd inside the Alamodome. Michael Cole welcomed him to the commentary booth as Corey Graves became furious.

He’s baaaaaaack! @PatMcAfeeShow is here on for the Men’s #RoyalRumble Match!

For those not in the know, Pat McAfee previously took a temporary leave from WWE to work for ESPN College GameDay. WWE talent had been anticipating his return at the Royal Rumble event.

Michael Cole previously stated that Pat McAfee revitalized his career as well. Stay tuned to Ringside News for more from the ongoing Royal Rumble premium live event.
