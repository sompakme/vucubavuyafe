Do you need a car to get around Cabo?

August 29, 2024
blog

It depends on where you plan to stay and what activities you have planned. If you’re staying in downtown Cabo San Lucas or San José del Cabo, you can easily explore on foot. However, if you want to visit attractions further away or have more flexibility in your exploration, renting a car is recommended. Here are some answers to common questions about getting around Cabo:

1. Are there Ubers in Cabo Mexico?

Yes, Uber and Lyft operate in Cabo San Lucas. However, they are still relatively new in the area and there have been some issues between rideshare drivers and taxi drivers. It is advisable to be aware of potential complications and consider alternative transportation options.

2. Can you walk around Cabo San Lucas?

Yes, Cabo San Lucas is a very walkable town and many attractions are within walking distance. If you prefer to explore on foot, you can easily do so in the downtown area.

3. Is Cabo San Lucas a walkable city?

Yes, Cabo San Lucas is a walkable city and it is small enough to explore on foot. You can easily access shops, restaurants, and nightlife without the need for a car.

4. How do tourists get around in Cabo?

Tourists in Cabo have several transportation options available to them. They can use certified DMCs (Destination Management Companies) that offer shuttles to hotels and resorts, rent a car, take a taxi, or use the local bus system. The choice depends on personal preference and the desired level of convenience.

5. Can you trust Uber in Cabo?

Using Uber in Cabo can be convenient, but there have been some issues with traffic stops and licensing, as well as tensions between Uber drivers and traditional taxi drivers. It is advisable to be cautious and consider alternative transportation options.

6. How much is taxi in Cabo?

The cost of a taxi in Cabo San Lucas can vary depending on the distance traveled. On average, you can expect to pay around $8-10 USD for a one-mile trip within Cabo San Lucas, and around $35-40 USD for a trip from San Jose del Cabo to Cabo San Lucas. From the airport, the cost can be around $80 USD for a taxi to Cabo San Lucas in Zone 3.

7. How far is Los Cabos from the airport?

Los Cabos is located approximately 28 miles (about 20 minutes driving) from the international airport. The distance can vary depending on the specific location within Los Cabos.

8. How many days should I go to Cabo San Lucas?

The ideal duration for a trip to Cabo San Lucas can vary depending on personal preferences and the desired activities. Many guests stay for around a week, allowing enough time to explore popular tourist highlights, enjoy leisure activities, and experience the local culture. However, the duration can be shorter or longer depending on individual preferences.

9. How many days in Cabo is enough?

Many visitors find that staying in Cabo for at least four days allows them enough time to experience popular attractions, engage in activities, and also have time to relax on the beach. For a more comprehensive experience, staying for five or more days is recommended.

10. Is it safe to rent a car in Cabo San Lucas?

Yes, for the majority of travelers, Cabo San Lucas is considered safe for renting a car. It is advisable to be prepared and take necessary precautions, such as understanding local driving laws, securing proper insurance coverage, and being aware of your surroundings. Renting a car can provide convenience and flexibility in exploring the area.

11. Is it easy to get around Cabo?

Getting around Cabo is relatively easy with various transportation options available. These include Uber, taxis, rental cars, private shuttles, and local buses. Each option offers its own advantages and can be chosen based on individual preferences and convenience.

12. How much is a taxi from Los Cabos airport to Cabo San Lucas?

Taxis from the Los Cabos airport to Cabo San Lucas generally cost a minimum of $80 USD for up to four people. Additional charges of $20 per person may apply for larger groups. Shared airport shuttles can be a more affordable alternative for couples or solo travelers.

13. How do you get rides in Cabo?

There are several ways to get around in Cabo, including using shuttles provided by hotels and resorts, renting a car, taking a taxi, or using the local bus system. Additionally, certified DMCs offer transportation services for tourists.

14. Do I need a car for Cabo San Lucas?

A car is not necessary for getting around Cabo San Lucas, as there are many transportation options available. Public transportation is easy and affordable, and there are shuttles provided by hotels and resorts for added convenience. However, renting a car can be convenient if you plan to visit areas that are not easily accessible by public transportation or if you prefer more flexibility in your itinerary.

15. Is it worth getting all-inclusive in Cabo?

Whether or not an all-inclusive package is worth it depends on personal preferences and travel style. All-inclusive deals can be a good option for those who want a relaxing vacation with plenty of downtime. If you prefer a more adventurous or culturally immersive experience, an all-inclusive package may not be the best choice as it may limit your exploration of local dining and activities.

16. Do you need cash in Cabo?

While many restaurants and stores in Cabo accept credit cards, it is advisable to carry cash for small establishments or for situations where cards may not be accepted. Cash can also be useful for bargaining or getting better prices. However, it is important to note that paying with a credit card offers fraud protection, while cash payments do not.

17. Is it safe to drive from Cabo to La Paz?

Driving from Cabo to La Paz is generally safe, as the roads are well-maintained. The trip takes approximately two hours, and it is advisable to have someone else drive if you are not familiar with driving in Mexico. However, it is always important to exercise caution and adhere to local traffic laws and conditions.

18. Is Cabo very touristy?

Yes, Cabo San Lucas, along with San José del Cabo, is a popular tourist destination in Mexico. It attracts a significant number of tourists and offers a wide range of tourist amenities, including beaches, resorts, restaurants, and nightlife.

19. Are taxis easy to get in Cabo?

Yes, taxis are easily available in Cabo San Lucas. They are usually large vans that can accommodate multiple passengers. Taxis can be found throughout the city and are a convenient means of transportation for tourists.

20. How many days is Cabo from Cancun?

Cabo and Cancun are located on opposite coasts of Mexico. The distance between the two destinations is significant, with a flight from Cabo to Cancun taking several hours. The duration of travel can vary depending on the chosen mode of transportation.

21. How many days is Cabo from Puerto Vallarta?

The distance between Cabo and Puerto Vallarta is significant, and the travel time can vary depending on the chosen mode of transportation. By air, the flight between the two destinations takes approximately two hours. By road, it can take over 20 hours, as it requires travel through several states.

22. Why is Cabo better than Cancun?

Both Cabo and Cancun offer unique experiences and have their own attractions. Cabo is known for its beautiful beaches, stunning natural landscapes, and vibrant nightlife. It is a popular destination for fishing and offers a more laid-back and relaxed atmosphere. On the other hand, Cancun is renowned for its white sandy beaches, Mayan ruins, and a larger variety of resorts, restaurants, and entertainment options.
