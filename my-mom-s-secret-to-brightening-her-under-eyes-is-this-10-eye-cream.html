My Mom's Secret to Brightening Her Under-Eyes Is This $10 Eye Cream

August 01, 2024
blog

For as long as my mom can remember, she’s battled with persistent dark circles; her long overnight shifts working at the post office only furthered the darkened state of her under-eyes. Always being proactive about her skincare routine, she was determined to find a solution in the perfect sensitive-skin friendly eye cream that could camouflage the effects of sleep deprivation by depuffing, hydrating, and brightening her under-eye area. After much searching, she discovered Derma-E’s Hydrating Eye Cream, which has been a game-changer in her morning routine, offering a much-needed boost to her tired eyes — and she plans on stocking up while it’s on sale for $10.

The anti-aging eye cream is packed with tons of nourishing ingredients including hyaluronic acid, which is known for its ability to attract and retain moisture within the skin, while nourishing delicate eyelids and under-eyes. It also contains vitamin A, which accelerates the removal of dull skin cells for a more youthful appearance, vitamin C to firm, lift, and smooth, and vitamin E, which leaves skin soft and supple. There’s also green tea to protect against sun damage and fight back against signs of aging. It’s suitable for sensitive skin being free of parabens and sulfates and 100 percent vegan.

Derma-E Hydrating Eye Cream

My mom says Derma-E’s Eye Cream made her “under-eyes look and feel hydrated, more lifted, and much brighter” immediately upon application. “It’s taken years off [her] raccoon-looking dark circles,” she said. She squeezes out a few dots a half-inch below her eyes and gently massages them in using her ring finger. After a month of consistent use, she “no longer wakes up with excessive puffiness under [her] eyes” and “there’s been a noticeable difference in how bright [her] under-eyes look.” My mom also mentioned that she “looks more awake even if [she hasn’t] gotten much sleep” — it gives “the illusion of eight hours of sleep in a tube.”

My mom added that the eye cream “acts like a filter” and is “very blurring” when it comes to hiding her fine lines and has even eliminated others altogether. She even started using it as a spot treatment on other parts of her face, like on her smile lines and around her forehead. My mom also says the consistency of the cream is lightweight, non-greasy, and melts down into the skin effortlessly. After hours of wear, she doesn’t experience any irritation and her makeup applies smoothly over top with no pilling.
