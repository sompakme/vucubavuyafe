Popular Nollywood actress, Tayo Sobola aka Sotayo Gaga has shared good news with fans and colleagues on social media.

The actress took to her official Instagram page to share videos from the moment she was conferred with the title of Saraunia which means queen mother in Uke Kingdom in Nasarawa state.

In the first clip, the movie star who donned a white attire, headgear, and blue scarf sat on a mat in front of the king and rose to a kneeling position when another man trapped her in a white hooded robe.
