AFTER being in front of the cameras for years, it's no surprise that Jersey Shore cast members have transformed their look - some more than others.

Alongside Angelina Pivarnick, who looks almost unrecognizable these days, JWoww has changed a lot - though she still claims that the only procedures she's had done have been two boob jobs, lip fillers, and Botox.

PRAYING MANTIS

2009

When Jenni first stepped in front of the cameras, she was just 23 years old and she dreamed of becoming an animator for Disney or Pixar.

However, things changed as she auditioned to be part of the Jersey Shore cast, a show that is still running to this day.

Back then, Jenni claimed that whenever she was done with a guy, she would bite their heads off – and she was not afraid to swing her fist against men or women in the show.

The stunning castmate rocked her hair dark with side sweep bangs, and still enjoyed tanning obsessively.

At this point, Jenni had already undergone breast augmentation surgery, going from a B cup to an F cup.

REDHEAD JENNI

2010

By 2010, Jenni had begun to experiment with her hair color and went redhead for a few months.

She first tried steaks in her hair before dyeing it completely.

The bombshell also slimmed down as her face seemed more sculptured.

NEW STYLE

2012

By this year, Jenni had opted for a more subtle and sophisticated style as she stopped rocking icy-looking eyeshadow and her wardrobe consisted of darker and classic pieces.

She also got rid of the side-swept bangs.

FIRST TIME MAMA

2014

In 2014, Jenni gave birth to her first child with now-ex husband Roger, whom she met on the Jersey Shore.

The star kept a more natural look at this point and had yet to undergo any type of cosmetic procedure.

The only difference at this point was that she had treaded her red hair for blonde highlights.

BOTOX?

2015

Following the birth of her daughter Meilani, Radar Online wrote that it was possible the star got more than just baby weight on her face as she looked plumper and smoother.

It's possible that she also changed the shape of her eyebrows with the Botox as she looked to have a more awake look to her eyes.

WHOA MAMA

2017

By this year, Jenni was a mom of two and she looked absolutely amazing.

The Jersey Shore star sowed off a slimmer nose, though she claims it's her original nose, a more chiseled face, and plumper cheeks which could be the work of fillers.

Her lips also looked plumper and more shapely.

Additionally, she had undergone a second boob job to remove the old implants and put in new ones.

She claimed that after breastfeeding two kids, she was unhappy with her breasts and was encouraged by her husband Roger to get new ones.

To this day, Jenni only confirms she's had two breast surgeries and fillers.

SINGLE WOMAN

2019

By 2019, Jenni had divorced after years of unhappiness.

At this time, she rocked some of her most daring outfits, looking to show him what he was missing.

She also slimmed down throughout most of her body, which could have been due to the custody war she had with her now-ex.

CHEEKY GIRL

2020

In 2020, Jenni's face had shifted once more with rumors that she'd had cheek fillers.

The apple of her cheeks seemed fuller, while the rest of her face looked slimmer - a possible sign of a cosmetic procedure.

The timing would have been great as the world shut down in 2020 due to the ongoing Covid-19 pandemic.

NEW ENGAGEMENT RING

2021

 This year, the star looked very different with more lip fillers to create a Kylie Jenner-like pout.
