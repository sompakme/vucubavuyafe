Holly Sonders shocks fans after posting video of what she looked like before surgery

September 20, 2024
blog

Holly Sonders' radical switch to explicit content creator tends to overshadow her earlier success in golf and sports journalism. Many people forget that she was once one of the most popular personalities in the sphere of golf.

Having had a short yet accomplished junior golfing career, competing thrice in the NCAA Regional tournament and two times in the NCAA Championship tournament, Sonders rose to fame in broadcasting, most notably as a reporter and TV host with Fox Sports.

In more recent times, though, Sonders has made the switch to explicit content, prioritizing her social media brand over her career as a Jounalist. In fact, the former presenter's principal project right now is Exposed Sports, which in Sonders' words, involves your "favorite IG babes going head to head in competition... sweaty and oiled up".

Sonders to return to TV?

In the influencer's most recent Instagram post, she announced some bombshell news regarding her future.

Sonders uploaded a throwback video from ten years ago, in which she hinted at a possible return to TV. "Playing Lessons with the Pros was by far my favorite show I hosted...we traveled to the best golfers in the world, and I played their home courses one-on-one with them. The footage we got was amazing and the show was super popular. After 5 years off TV I'm feeling like it might be time for a return..." she wrote in the caption.

Eye-catching physical change

It wasn't the news of a possible return to the screen that shocked Sonders' Instagram followers. Instead, it was her eye-catching physical change. Sonders looked totally unrecognizable in the footage which was filmed before all her multiple plastic surgery operations.

Fans took to the comments to express their complete and utter shock at her physical transformation. "Back when you were a natural beauty," wrote one user. "Back when you were the most attractive women in the business..." said another.
