Metro Boomin Teases Young Thug Music With New Countdown Timer

September 03, 2024
blog

Last night, you probably saw that Gunna dropped his new album a Gift & a Curse. Subsequently, Metro Boomin dropped a QR code on his Twitter and Instagram accounts. This was then captioned "BUSINESS IS BUSINESS" with a Young Thug tag. If you were to scan the QR code, you would see that this takes you to a new website with a countdown timer. In just over five days from now, some new music is going to be dropping. This has led to all sorts of speculation about whether or not we are getting an album or a song.

Metro Boomin Wants Some More

Moreover, plenty of artists also took to their social media to promote this new music. Drake, Lil Durk, Wheezy, Coi Leray, and many others have been showing love by also posting the QR code. It should be noted that none of these artists have shared the new Gunna album. Clearly, there is some hesitancy to support the artist given the snitch allegations. Although he addressed them on his new album, some are already looking to this new Thugger collab.

Between now and this future release, it remains to be seen whether or not we will get any more details about it. For now, however, it is clear that the speculation will be rampant.
