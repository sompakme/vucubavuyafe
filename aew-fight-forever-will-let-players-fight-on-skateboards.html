AEW Fight Forever Will Let Players Fight On Skateboards

September 02, 2024
blog

AEW’s announcement about its venture into the video game industry was a very welcome one, as it was the first time since TNA’s game in 2008 that WWE games will no longer go unchallenged. Fight Forever will release soon and it appears the game will pet players fight on skateboards.

AEW: Fight Forever’ had been hit with a number of delays since its announcement in November 2020. This has made fans believe was going to be stuck in developmental hell forever, but that wasn’t the case.

As fans know by now, it was officially announced that AEW: Fight Forever will be releasing on June 29th on all modern consoles as well as on PC. The game will also feature ton of blood, which is an enticing aspect for many fans.

AEW Fight Forever will be an arcade-style of wrestling game and is primarily made with fan enjoyment in mind. With that, it appears that the game will also let players fight each other on skateboards in a whacky mode.

The game will also include very unique feature for fans. We will have to wait and see how AEW Fight Forever is received by gamers, especially after how the WWE 2K series has been able to redeem itself for the past couple of years.
