T. Graham Brown (born October 30, 1954, Arabi, Georgia), sometimes known as Anthony Graham Brown, is an American country music singer who went by the stage name T. Graham Brown. Brown has been recording studio albums since 1986 and has had more than twenty songs charted on the Billboard Hot Country Songs charts throughout that time period. There were three of these singles that reached Number One — “Hell and High Water” from 1986, “Don’t Go to Strangers” from 1988, and “Darlene” from 1989 — and eight more that reached the Top Ten.
