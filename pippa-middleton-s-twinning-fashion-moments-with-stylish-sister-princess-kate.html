Pippa Middleton's twinning fashion moments with stylish sister Princess Kate

July 17, 2024
blog

The Middleton sisters have been known to share their clothes with each other

    The Princess of Wales has long been the pinnacle of royal style perfection. Impeccably dressed, with an archive of perfectly tailored pieces and timeless garments, Princess Kate has mastered the ultimate balance between sartorial queen and follower of royal etiquette. But did you know her sister Pippa Middleton is equally a trendsetter? 

    The Middleton sisters have shared everything from a flat together, to their wedding days, to their passion for athletics, so it only makes sense that Pippa and Kate also occasionally share a wardrobe. 

    Though Pippa's style tends to be far more relaxed than her royal sister's, there have been several occasions where the sisterly duo have stepped out in outfits so similar we've done a double take. 

    Kate and Pippa's matching engagement dresses

    Princess Kate's navy dress worn to announce her engagement to Prince William remains one of her most iconic style moments in the royal spotlight. Yet Kate's now-famous Issa silk wrap dress was actually worn by Pippa first. 

    Pippa appeared to wear a red version of the silk wrap dress to a book launch in 2007. If not the same designer, there's no denying Kate took style notes from her younger sister when she chose her flattering frock for her post-proposal interview.

    Kate and Pippa twinning in fur

    The stylish Middleton sisters kept warm with matching fur hats this winter - even their mother Carole Middleton has one too.

    Kate and Pippa's matching maternity style

    Whilst pregnant with Prince Louis, the Princess of Wales looked radiant rocking one of her favourite designers, Catherine Walker. 

    Pippa clearly took inspiration from her sister for her own maternity style, stepping out in the same year in a near-identical emerald dress during her pregnancy with her son Arthur. 

    Kate and Pippa's designer wedding style

    Sister, sister! The Princess of Wales chose her sister Pippa to be by her side on the most historic day of her royal career - her wedding day. The lookalike sisters looked angelic in bridal white on the big day, both wearing custom designs by Sarah Burton for Alexander McQueen. 

    Kate and Pippa's polkadot perfection

    Either the sisters have style telepathy, or this is proof they really do share clothes. Back in 2018, Princess Kate stepped out for a royal engagement wearing a flattering maroon dress from Whistles, adorned with white polkadots and pearlescent buttons. Sure enough, Pippa stepped out wearing the exact same dress just a week later. What a pair! 

    Kate and Pippa's Wimbledon shades

    Princess Kate and Pippa both rocked oversized tinted sunglasses to watch a nail-biting men's singles final at Wimbledon in 2012.

    Kate and Pippa's summer style

    Pippa looked perfect in a pretty Anna Mason embroidered dress at Wimbledon in 2018, pairing her look with wedge espadrilles from Penelope Chilvers, and a panama hat by royal milliner Jess Collett. 

    Her royal sister is also a fan of broderie anglaise, rocking a near-identical fitted frock from Zimmerman during her Australian tour.
