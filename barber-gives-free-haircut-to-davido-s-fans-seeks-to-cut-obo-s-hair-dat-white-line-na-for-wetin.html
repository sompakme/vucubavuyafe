  • He wishes to cut the hair of the Afrobeats singer, but he has yet to get the opportunity, and this spurred him to adopt an initiative
  • The idea has been well-received by fans of the singer, who prayed for the barber's dreams to come through

A barber, Kelvin Classic, has captured the hearts of many fans of singer David Adeleke, aka Davido, as he cuts their hair for free.

Kelvin desires to cut the hair of the Unavailable crooner and the perfect way to get his attention is to create awareness about his desire.

He placed a chair and a table beside a road where he had a pink cardboard displaying his handwork and what he intends to achieve.
